# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background,
identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
